Welcome to Blue star

At Blue Star Cafe, coffee isn't just a drink; it's a craft. We partner directly with sustainable farms around the globe to select premium beans, ensuring fair wages for farmers and top-tier quality for you.

Our master roasters highlight the unique flavor profiles of every origin, serving up everything from bold, rich espresso blends to crisp, vibrant pour-overs tailored to your taste.

More than a cafe, Blue Star is a vibrant community hub in the heart of Dhaka city.
